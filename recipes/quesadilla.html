Quesadilla

Recipe

Although the picture shows beef filled quesadillas, you can use whatever you have in your fridge to make these yummy boys.

Ingredients

  • Two large tortillas
  • A bunch of shredded cheese. Any kind you like
  • Whatever other fillings you want: beef, chicken, peppers, etc.
  • Sasla/sour cream for dipping
  • Butter/oil

Steps

  1. Add butter/oil to pan and heat over medium-high
  2. Once hot, add one tortilla to pan
  3. Top with cheese, desired fillings, and other tortilla
  4. Cook until bottom tortilla is golden brown
  5. Flip and cook until the other side is browned and cheese is melted
  6. Remove and cut into quarters
  7. Dip, dip, dip those bad boys and enjoy
