Chili Dog

Recipe

This chili dog recipe will knock your socks off. You're gonna want to chow down on the whole pack of these puppies once you see and smell the finished product.

Back when I was in elementary school, my mom would make one of these bad boys for me every day after school. That's how I got the nickname "Chili Boy" in the neighborhood. Looking back, that may have been why I didn't have many friends growing up and why I ballooned up to 200 lbs as a 12 year old, but on to the recipe...

Ingredients

  • Pack of 100% beef hotdogs
  • Pack of hot dog buns
  • One can of chili without beans
  • One medium red onion
  • 6 oz of cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Dice the medium red onion and set aside
  2. Grate the cheddar cheese and set aside
  3. Heat up the chili on the stove over medium heat
  4. Cook hotdogs using preferred method, grill, microwave, boil etc.
  5. Place cooked hotdog in bun. Add warmed chili, cheese, and onions
  6. Enjoy and try to forget about childhood trauma
