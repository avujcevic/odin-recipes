Minon Egg

Recipe

Delight adults and children alike with these cute minion eggs. Bob is my favorite. Which one is yours?

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Yellow food coloring
  • Blue food coloring
  • Food safe marker
  • Googly eyes

Steps

  1. Hard boil the eggs. I don't feel like typing it out so look that up yourself
  2. Dip one side in the blue food coloring, then the yellow
  3. Stick on one or two googly eyes depending on the minion
  4. Draw on a mouth and goggles with the marker
